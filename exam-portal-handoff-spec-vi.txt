TAI LIEU HANDOFF DU AN
ExamPortal

1. THONG TIN DU AN

Ten du an: ExamPortal
Ten repo/folder: exam-portal
Frontend: React
Backend: Spring Boot
Co so du lieu: SQL Server

Dinh huong hien tai:
Tap trung lam that tot he thong thi online truoc.
Phan challenge va leaderboard de giai doan sau, khong dua vao pham vi chinh cua version hien tai.


2. TONG QUAN DU AN

ExamPortal la mot ung dung web full-stack cho he thong thi trac nghiem truc tuyen.

Day khong chi la mot website quan ly. Nghiep vu chinh dien ra truc tiep trong he thong:
- giang vien tao cau hoi va de thi
- hoc sinh dang nhap va lam bai truc tiep tren web
- he thong tu dong cham diem
- hoc sinh xem ket qua
- giang vien va admin theo doi bai lam va thong ke

Trong tam nghiep vu:
- tao de thi
- mo lich thi
- cho hoc sinh lam bai online
- tu dong cham diem
- quan ly ket qua

Phan mo rong de sau:
- challenge cong khai
- leaderboard / bang xep hang

17. LOGIC TINH DIEM

Ban dau nen tinh diem theo cach don gian:
- moi cau dung = 1 diem
- cau sai = 0 diem
- bo trong = 0 diem

Co the hien thi:
- diem goc = so cau dung
- diem chuan hoa = correct_answers / total_questions * 10
